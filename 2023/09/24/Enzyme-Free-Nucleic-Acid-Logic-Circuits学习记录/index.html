Enzyme-Free Nucleic Acid Logic Circuits

介绍

生物使用生化电路来表征信息，处理任务，它们非常高效

电子器件基于数字逻辑使用硅基的门电路来表征处理信息。

文章实现了基于DNA的数字逻辑电路:demonstrate AND, OR, and NOT gates, signal restoration,amplification,feedback,and cascading. Gate design and circuit construction is modular

DNA电路的输入:singlestranded nucleic acids

DNA电路的输出:singlestranded nucleic acids或microRNAs

1

现在man-made chemical circuits无法与硅基电路的可靠复杂相比，但是在这一领域一旦建立起可靠的规则，可以有很多的用途（常规的应用）：

  • 体外控制纳米级装置
  • 分析复杂化学样品
  • 与生物体现存的电路接口

2

DNA电路原理:WatsonCrick base pairing

几种电路实现方法

  1. Allosteric ribozymes(一种核糖酶)

    perform logical functions

    优点:Allosteric ribozymes that take small molecules as input have been shown to perform logical functions

    缺点:their output (a cleaved or ligated oligonucleotide) is of a different form than the input因为输入输出形式的不同，导致级联是困难的，很难构建复杂电路

  2. using deoxyribozymes controlled by input oligonucleotides(另一种受控制的酶(被输入寡核苷酸控制))

    可以构建:Automata performing multiple logical operations in parallel (4), single-step signaling cascades (5), and a feedback cycle that acts as an exponential chain reaction (6) were built

  3. uses sequence recognition to control enzyme catalysis of covalent bond formation and breakage (8–10).(利用序列识别控制共价键形成和断裂的酶催化)

  4. nucleic acid reactions can be driven without enzyme or (deoxy)ribozyme catalysis(在没有酶的情况下反应)

    this principle has been exploited to construct DNA-based logic gates and signaling cascades (13, 14).

  5. hybridization and conformational changes(杂化和构象变化)

    Recently, engineered nucleic acid logic switches based on hybridization and conformational changes have been successfully demonstrated in vivo (15, 16).

挑战:设计化学门电路构建大型复杂的电路

关键:digital design principles

3

电路总体设计的一些要点:

  • 构建DNA电路，体现了digital design principles： logic, cascading, restoration, fan-out, and modularity

  • 使用short oligonucleotides（短寡核苷酸）作为输入和输出（因为输入输出形式相同，可以方便的构建复杂电路，进行级联）

  • 使用浓度的高低代表逻辑值1和0

  • Signal restoration is performed by threshold and amplifier gates that protect against noise, signal loss, and leaky reactions.(信号恢复是由阈值和放大器门执行，防止噪声，信号损失和泄漏反应)

  • Amplifier gates can also be used to ensure that a logic gate generates sufficient signal to drive multiple downstream targets.(放大器门也可以用来确保逻辑门产生足够的信号来驱动多个下游目标)

一些特点：

  • Gate function is entirely determined by base pairing and breaking
  • Every gate consists of one or more gate strands and one output strand
  • 一个门电路的输出链可以作为另一个门电路的输入链或者为外界通过一个输出provide a readout in a fluorescence experiment(类似于电脑的显示屏等输出设备)
  • Both ends of the output strand (Fig. 1A), or only one end (translator gates in Fig. 2), can be attached to the gate complex
  • Each gate strand contains a recognition region that is complementary to its input.(每个门链包含一个与其输入互补的识别区域)
  • 在刚开始反应时 the recognition regions of all gate strands are doublestranded and therefore inert, except for the toehold farthest from the output strand (strand G in Fig. 1A)（所有栅链的识别区域都是双链的，因此是惰性的，除了距离输出链最远的支点）
  • When the first input binds this toehold, it displaces the first gate strand by three-way branch migration (18, 19), exposing the toehold for the subsequent input and releasing an inert double-stranded waste product.(当第一个输入结合该支点时，它通过三向分支迁移取代第一门链(18,19)，为后续输入暴露支点并释放惰性双链废物。)，另一个输入的过程也是类似的
  • The output strand is released if and only if both inputs are present(当且仅当两个输入都存在时才释放输出链)
  • DNA sequences (tables S1 to S3) were selected to ensure correct complementarity while minimizing spurious interactions (20)(选择DNA序列(表S1至S3)以确保正确的互补，同时最大限度地减少虚假相互作用(20))

4

在构建多级门电路时 It is essential that the output strand not interact with downstream gates before release.

方法:Protecting the toehold binding region of output strands in upstream gates prevents such interactions.

built a circuit composed of one AND gate and two translator gates that demonstrates this principle

translator :A translator gate converts the signal encoded in the input strand to the signal encoded in the output strand and is implemented as a single-input AND gate.

translator的例子:The translator gates JK and LM translate two biological microRNA sequences (mouse let7c and miR-124a) into outputs with recognition regions identical to strands Gin and Fin.

translator使用的条件:The input to a translator gate and the recognition region of its output strand need only share sequence in the toehold region.

full translator :If two translators are cascaded, then there is no sequence restriction between the initial input strand and the final output strand. This is called a full translator;

一些发现:

  • the circuit functions well in the presence of potentially interfering biological RNA (mouse brain total RNA) at a concentration in excess of gate complexes and input strands

  • Increasing the temperature to 37°C does not degrade circuit performance

6

使用一些少数的门电路就可以构建完整的逻辑函数

Logical OR: functionality is obtained by using two gates that produce the same output

If Boolean values are represented by the presence of either one strand (“0”) or another strand (“1”)—the so-called “dual-rail” representation (21)—then AND and OR are themselves sufficient to compute any Boolean function

If a Boolean value is represented by the presence or absence of a single input strand, a NOT gate may be necessary

NOT gate:The NOT gate makes use of an additional “inverter” strand that triggers the gate unless the input strand is present to act as a competitive inhibitor

7

A gate may fail in two ways:

  • It may fail to produce enough output when triggered,
  • or it may “leak” by spontaneously releasing the output strand.

解决方法:

Both types of error require signal restoration; the former requires increasing a moderate output amount to the full activation level, and the latter requires decreasing a small output amount to a negligible level.

8

As increasingly larger circuits are constructed, speed becomes a limiting factor

解决方法:

Speeding up the responses of individual gates (e.g., by shortening recognition domains) or changing other reaction conditions may improve overall circuit performance.

总结

Our success in creating large circuits can be attributed to adherence to the tenets of digital logic, toehold sequestering combined with branch migration and strand displacement, reduction of leak reactions by purification, and modularity of design. The logic gates developed here and the principles on which they are based can also be used to construct analog or hybrid circuits (24) and are likely to prove compatible with other approaches to building molecular automata in vitro and in vivo（我们在制造大型电路方面的成功可归功于坚持数字逻辑的原则，结合分支迁移和链位移的支点隔离，通过净化减少泄漏反应，以及设计的模块化。这里开发的逻辑门及其所基于的原理也可用于构建模拟或混合电路(24)，并且可能被证明与体外和体内构建分子自动机的其他方法兼容）
